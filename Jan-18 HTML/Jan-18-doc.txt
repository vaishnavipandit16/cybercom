------------------------Arrow Functions------------------------

-->Arrow functions do not have the this kryword.
-->They simply use the this keyword of the function they are returning.So they have a lexical this keyword.
-->Only in a method call, this keyword actually points to that object.But in a regular function call this keyword points to the global object.
